What is BMI?

Body mass index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight that applies to adult men and women.

Measure types

There are two principal measure types:

  • Standard: the measures are in pounds & inches
  • Metric: kilograms & meters

Instructions

You'll have to enter weight and height using standard or metric measures (to make this use the combo and select the desired option). Then press the button to submit and see the results.
